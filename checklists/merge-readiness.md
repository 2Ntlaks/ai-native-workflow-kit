# Merge Readiness

Use as the final gate before accepting or merging agent work.

- [ ] Acceptance criteria are met.
- [ ] Tests passed, or gaps are explained and accepted.
- [ ] Security-sensitive areas received the right review.
- [ ] Review packet is complete.
- [ ] Follow-up work is separated into required-before-merge and optional-later.
- [ ] Human review focus was addressed.
- [ ] The final diff is small enough to understand.
