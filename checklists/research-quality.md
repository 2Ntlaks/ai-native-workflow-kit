# Research Quality

Use before trusting a research spike or tool-behavior claim.

- [ ] Research question is answered directly.
- [ ] Primary or official sources are used, or the lack of them is stated as a caveat.
- [ ] Important claims are added to `research/claims-index.md` or a source card.
- [ ] Caveats and source limits are visible.
- [ ] Tool-specific capabilities are marked `verify before use` unless confirmed.
- [ ] Recommendation is tied to a decision the human needs to make.
