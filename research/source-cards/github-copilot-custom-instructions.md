## Key Claims

- Copilot supports several instruction mechanisms depending on surface.
- Repository-wide instructions can live in .github/copilot-instructions.md.
- Copilot may not follow custom instructions identically every time.

## Caveats

- Support differs across GitHub.com, VS Code, JetBrains, Visual Studio, Xcode, and other surfaces.

## Workflow Implications

- The Copilot adapter is concise and warns that support varies.
- Copilot instructions are treated as guidance, not enforcement.
- Review still depends on checklists and human inspection.
