## Pass Threshold

The workflow is healthy enough to reuse when:

- Prompt quality is 4 or 5.
- Clarification loops are 0-1.
- Rework is none or small.
- Verification evidence is enough for the task risk.
- The human can review the result faster than doing the task alone.
